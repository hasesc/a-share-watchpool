# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

A股观察池 · A-Share Watchpool is maintained as a public market data research, paper simulation, and strategy audit project. The community should remain focused on reproducible research workflows, documentation, examples, tests, and safe open-source maintenance.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions.
- Keep feedback specific, evidence-based, and relevant to the repository.
- Use fictional, anonymized, or public data when discussing examples.
- Respect the project boundary: public data research, paper simulation, and strategy audit only.
- Report sensitive data exposure carefully, without reposting secrets or private account information.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Posting API keys, cookies, tokens, real account data, or real trading records.
- Asking contributors to connect broker accounts, place orders, or bypass data-service terms.
- Presenting repository output as investment advice, stock recommendations, follow-trading guidance, or return promises.
- Pressuring maintainers or contributors for real trading decisions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and issues that violate this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocking further participation in the repository.

## Reporting

If you see a conduct issue, open a GitHub issue with the minimum necessary context. Do not include secrets, private account information, or real trading records in the report.

For sensitive-data concerns, follow [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
